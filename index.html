Flagship · case study

Blueprint

Steel-to-task operations for grocery construction.

Design system SvelteKit 2 · Svelte 5 MongoDB Native iOS Azure
Challenge

A fixtures-and-construction shop ran quoting, field work, and the books across disconnected spreadsheets, email, and QuickBooks — no single view from lead to ledger.

Approach

One platform spanning the whole job lifecycle — prospecting → quotes → a field Kanban → double-entry books → forecasting — designed and built solo on a token-driven, accessible design system, web and native iOS.

Outcome

A single source of truth where email and receipts parse onto cards via AI. Live in production on Azure, backed by 560+ automated tests and a WCAG contrast gate enforced in CI.

▦

Job Board

Ranked Kanban with store lanes, aging & WIP signals, checklists, bulk actions, and a quick-add flow.

◴

Dashboard

Pipeline, win-rate, and cash-on-hand at a glance — Chart.js datasets themed for light and dark.

$

Books

In-house double-entry accounting: ledger, AR/AP, statements, bank rec, job costing, even fixed-asset depreciation.

❝

Quotes

Estimate builder with labor/total toggles and one-click branded PDFs; won quotes flow into invoices.

⌖

Prospects

Live warehouse leads from OpenStreetMap + county GIS — owner, occupant, and assessed value, no paid API.

∿

Forecast

Monte-Carlo revenue forecast running on hand-authored WebAssembly — a genuine ~2× over the JS twin.
